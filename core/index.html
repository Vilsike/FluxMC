Core Loop

Progress is political. You grow power through territory, projects, trade leverage, and public outcomes.

Form or join a House

Choose a banner and identity. Align with allies. Decide what your House is known for.

Claim and build

Establish territory and a seat of power. Infrastructure is influence, not decoration.

Earn Influence

Trade, govern, negotiate, complete initiatives, and shape outcomes that the realm sees.

Declare war

War is scheduled and objective-based. If you declare, you commit. Consequences should last.
